WHAT FACTORS DRIVE Robu ROBU Tech Fest 2022?

Science Fiction

ROBU Tech Fest 2022, one of the largest techno-management fests of Bangladesh, is on an odyssey to stretch out one's imaginations to the far reaches, and extract the technology that one sees in their mind's eye.

Out-of-box

Team ROBU aims at breaking down boundaries and thinking out of the box. After all, science fiction is all about letting your imagination run wild and capturing them into spectacular inventions.

Social Responsibility

With the world moving at a breakneck pace, it is important to ensure that everyone moves together. Team ROBU recognizes the need to uphold our social values and virtues to achieve true development.
